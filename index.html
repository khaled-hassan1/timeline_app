Who

We Are?

Established in 2010, TIMELINE Production and Events Company has emerged as a premier media production and event management firm based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.

Our journey began with a vision to revolutionize the media and event industries through innovative solutions and unparalleled services.

Over the past 15 years, we have consistently delivered exceptional results, earning a reputation for excellence and reliability.

Our Mission

Excellence in providing services using the latest equipment with creative minds that understand the needs of our partners and meet their aspirations and give them the best quality and originality.

Our Vision

To be the best and most reliable production choice in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

PRE-PRODUCTION

Research and Planning

Gather comprehensive information about Riyadh Valley Co.’s history, achievements, and future projects. Conduct meetings with key stakeholders to understand their vision and expectations.

Script Development

Develop a detailed script that narrates the story of Riyadh Valley Co., highlighting the main points to be covered. The script will undergo reviews and approvals to ensure it aligns with the company’s message and tone.

Storyboard Creation

Create a storyboard that visually represents the script. This will outline each scene and shot, providing a clear blueprint for the video production.

Logistics and Scheduling

Plan the logistics for video shoots, including locations, schedule, and necessary permissions. Arrange for equipment and crew required for the shoot.

PRODUCTION

Filming

Conduct video shoots at various locations, including Riyadh Valley Co.'s facilities, project sites, and offices. Capture high-quality footage of the company’s environment, activities, and products.

Interviews

Record interviews with key personnel, stakeholders, and clients. These interviews will provide authentic insights and add a personal touch to the video.

B-Roll Footage

Capture supplementary footage (B-roll) to enhance the narrative and provide context. This includes shots of the work environment, team interactions, and project highlights.

POST-PRODUCTION

Editing

Edit the captured footage to create a coherent and engaging storyline. This includes cutting, sequencing, and enhancing the visual and audio quality.

Visual Enhancements

Add animations, infographics, and other visual elements to highlight key information and make the video visually appealing.

Sound Design

Incorporate background music, sound effects, and voiceovers to enhance the overall impact of the video.

Finalization

Complete the final edit, ensuring all elements are polished and the video is ready for distribution.

Review and Feedback

Present the initial cut to Riyadh Valley Co. for review and feedback. Make necessary revisions to ensure the final video meets expectations.

OUR SERVICES – TIME LAPSE

Construction Timelapse

We use our own studio and AI-based techniques to produce long-term timelapse footage of construction projects, offering unmatched visual quality and detail.

Drone Filming

Drones offer dynamic, efficient, and flexible timelapse footage of construction sites, capturing wide views and hard-to-reach areas with ease.

Photography

Progress Documentary

Interior Timelapse

Showcasing the transformation of interior spaces through elegant time-lapse storytelling—perfect for highlighting the evolution of your design projects.
